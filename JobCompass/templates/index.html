What the system does — under the hood

Designed for real hiring workflows, the pipeline uses structured parsing and deterministic logic to give reliable, actionable outputs:

Resume Parsing
Extracts technical & soft skills, experiences, projects, education and certifications using a configurable LLM prompt or local heuristics.
Job Parsing
Categorizes critical vs beneficial skills and pulls years/levels from job descriptions to enable accurate matching.
Semantic Match
Weighted scoring (critical 60%, beneficial 20%, experience 15%, projects 5%) returns a 0–10 score and评级 (Excellent / Strong / Moderate / Weak).
Gap Analysis & Recommendation
Prioritized gaps (missing / transferable / learnable) and a single high-impact recommendation (reframe, highlight, add, or learn).
